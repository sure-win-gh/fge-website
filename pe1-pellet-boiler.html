Product Overview

A compact powerhouse — engineered for the modern home

The Froling PE1 is a fully automatic residential wood pellet boiler that redefines what you should expect from biomass heating. Occupying just 0.38m² of floor space — roughly the same footprint as a standard kitchen unit — it is one of the smallest biomass boilers on the market, without any compromise on output, control or reliability.

Available in seven sizes from 7kW to 35kW, the PE1 is designed to replace an oil or gas boiler like-for-like, connecting directly to your existing central heating circuit. All models carry the EU A++ energy label, and every PE1 is controlled by Froling's Lambdatronic 3200 system with its 7-inch colour touchscreen — giving you full control from the boiler itself or remotely via the Froling Connect app.

The PE1 is eligible for the UK Government's Boiler Upgrade Scheme, providing a £5,000 grant for eligible homeowners in England and Wales when installed by an MCS-certified installer. Freedom Green Energy manage the grant application from start to finish.

PE1 Technology

Intelligent by design —
effortless to live with

Every aspect of the PE1 is engineered to maximise combustion efficiency, minimise emissions, and reduce the time you spend managing your heating to virtually zero.

Lambdatronic 3200 — 7" Touchscreen

Froling's Lambdatronic 3200 controller manages every aspect of combustion automatically. The 7-inch colour touchscreen gives you intuitive access to schedules, efficiency data, service reminders and heating circuit controls — all in one place at the boiler, or remotely via the Froling Connect app.

Broadband Lambda Probe

A precision broadband oxygen sensor continuously monitors flue gases during operation, allowing the Lambdatronic to fine-tune the air-to-fuel ratio in real time. The result is cleaner combustion, lower particulate emissions, and stable efficiency regardless of variations in pellet quality between deliveries.

WOS — Wood Optimisation System

The built-in Wood Optimisation System (WOS) automatically adapts combustion parameters to match the energy content and moisture profile of the current pellet batch. This self-calibrating intelligence ensures consistently efficient output and low emissions even as pellet characteristics vary between suppliers and seasons.

Automatic Ash Removal

The PE1's automatic sliding grate transfers combustion residue to a sealed ash container with no user intervention required. The closed container system prevents dust and odour from entering the boiler room. Depending on usage, the ash drawer only needs emptying every few weeks — comparable to emptying a kitchen bin.

Double Burnback Protection

Safety is built in at every level. The PE1 features a double-layer burnback protection system — a mechanical shut-off valve and thermal sensor working together to prevent flame propagation back into the fuel store. This certified safety system provides reliable protection without requiring any active monitoring from the homeowner.

Froling Connect — Remote & Smart Home

Monitor and control the PE1 remotely via the Froling Connect app on any smartphone. The system supports up to 18 heating circuits, 8 hot water tanks and 4 storage tanks, and integrates with Loxone smart home systems and Modbus building management platforms. Status notifications and fault alerts are delivered by SMS or email.

System Options

Optional modules for a
complete boiler room

The PE1 can be expanded with factory-integrated optional modules, turning the boiler into a complete, all-in-one heating centre — reducing installation time and keeping the plant room tidy.

DHW Tank Block

An integrated 122-litre domestic hot water cylinder (available on 7–20kW models) combines seamlessly with the boiler body in a single compact unit, eliminating the need for a separate cylinder in the plant room. Ideal for properties with limited space or for straightforward oil-to-pellet conversions.

Available on 7–20 kW models

Hydraulic Block

The hydraulic block integrates a heating circuit pump and motorised mixing valve directly into the boiler — significantly reducing the number of separate components and connections needed in the plant room. This keeps installation clean and makes commissioning faster, particularly during retrofits.

All models

Room Air-Independent Supply

An optional sealed air supply kit allows the PE1 to draw combustion air directly from outside, operating independently of the room's air quality and pressure. This is essential for highly airtight new-build and passive-house installations, and is also useful in plant rooms with poor ventilation or in restricted spaces.

Common Questions

Froling PE1
FAQs

What is the Froling PE1 pellet boiler?

The Froling PE1 is a fully automatic residential wood pellet boiler available in seven output sizes from 7kW to 35kW. It is A++ energy rated, features a 7-inch colour touchscreen, and occupies just 0.38m² of floor space — making it one of the most compact biomass boilers available. It is BUS grant eligible and connects directly to your existing heating system.

What size PE1 do I need for my home?

For most modern, well-insulated homes a PE1 10kW or 15kW is sufficient. Older or larger properties typically need a PE1 20kW or 25kW. The PE1 modulates down to part-load automatically, so modest oversizing is not harmful. Our technical team provides free heat loss assessments to confirm the correct model — call 0333 700 0275 or enquire online.

Is the PE1 eligible for the £5,000 BUS grant?

Yes. The Froling PE1 qualifies for the Boiler Upgrade Scheme (BUS) when installed by an MCS-certified installer in England or Wales — providing a £5,000 grant applied at the point of purchase. Freedom Green Energy are MCS certified and manage the full grant application process for you at no additional cost.

Can the PE1 replace my existing oil or gas boiler?

Yes. The Froling PE1 connects directly to your existing central heating pipework and is compatible with radiators, underfloor heating systems, and standard hot water cylinders. The main addition to your property is a pellet store — typically a hopper, bagged store or bulk silo depending on your available space. Our team designs the complete system.

How is the PE1 fuelled and how much storage do I need?

Wood pellets are fed automatically from the integrated on-board hopper or from an external bulk store via a vacuum suction system. For most homes, a bulk pellet store of 1,000–3,000kg refilled once or twice per year is typical. Freedom Green will assess your property and design a fuel storage solution that suits your space and usage pattern.

How often does the PE1 need maintenance?

An annual service by a HETAS-registered Froling engineer is recommended. The Lambdatronic 3200 logs all operational data and sends automatic service reminders. The ash drawer requires emptying every few weeks (about 10 minutes of work). Freedom Green can connect you with trained Froling service engineers throughout the UK.
