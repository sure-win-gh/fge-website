Froling Technology

Engineered to
think for itself

Every Froling pellet boiler is built around intelligent control systems that constantly optimise combustion, minimise emissions and maximise efficiency — so you don't have to.

Lambdatronic 3200 Controller

Froling's advanced combustion management system continuously monitors and adjusts air supply, fuel feed and temperatures to maintain peak efficiency across all operating conditions. Includes service reminders and full data logging.

Broadband Lambda Probe

A precision oxygen sensor in the flue continuously measures combustion gases, allowing the Lambdatronic to fine-tune air intake in real time. The result is cleaner combustion, lower emissions and consistent efficiency regardless of pellet quality variations.

WOS — Wood Optimisation System

The WOS system automatically adapts the boiler's combustion parameters to match the energy content and moisture profile of the current pellet batch — ensuring stable, efficient output even when fuel quality varies between deliveries.

Automatic Ash Removal

The fully automated ash removal and compaction system transfers ash from the combustion chamber to a collection drawer with minimal intervention required. Depending on usage, emptying is only needed every few weeks — making maintenance effortless.

7" Colour Touchscreen Display

The intuitive 7" touchscreen gives you full control of your heating system from the boiler itself. Set schedules, monitor efficiency data, check service status and adjust temperatures — all through a clear, easy-to-navigate interface.

Froling Connect — Smart Home Integration

Monitor and control your Froling boiler remotely via the Froling Connect app. Compatible with smart home systems including Amazon Alexa, Google Home and leading building management platforms — giving you complete control from anywhere.

Why Go Pellet

The case for biomass
pellet heating

Pellet boilers deliver the convenience of gas or oil heating with the carbon credentials of renewable energy — and with fuel costs typically 30–50% lower than oil, the financial case is compelling too.

Near-Carbon-Neutral Heating

Wood pellets are classified as a renewable fuel — the CO₂ released during combustion is offset by the carbon absorbed during the tree's growth. Switching from oil to pellets can cut your heating carbon footprint by over 90%.

Significantly Lower Running Costs

Wood pellet fuel consistently costs 30–50% less per kWh than heating oil and mains gas. Combined with A++ boiler efficiency and the £5,000 BUS grant, many customers achieve payback within 5–7 years.

Fully Automatic — No Daily Intervention

Pellets are fed automatically from a hopper or bulk store to the boiler. There's no loading fuel, no adjusting dampers and no cleaning the glass — just set your schedule and let the Lambdatronic manage the rest.

Compatible with Existing Systems

A Froling pellet boiler connects directly to your existing central heating circuit — radiators, underfloor heating, or hot water cylinders — making it a like-for-like replacement for oil or gas with minimal system changes required.

Common Questions

Pellet boiler
FAQs

What is a Froling wood pellet boiler?

A Froling pellet boiler is a fully automatic renewable heating system that burns compressed wood pellets to heat your home and hot water. Models range from 7kW residential units to 350kW commercial systems — all A++ rated and as simple to live with as a gas boiler.

Are Froling pellet boilers eligible for the BUS grant?

Yes. Froling pellet boilers installed by an MCS-certified installer qualify for the UK Government's Boiler Upgrade Scheme, providing a £5,000 grant in England and Wales. We handle the paperwork for you.

What size pellet boiler do I need?

For most well-insulated homes, a 10–20kW boiler is sufficient. Older or larger properties may need 20–35kW. Our team performs a full heat loss calculation to specify exactly the right model — at no cost to you.

Can a pellet boiler replace my oil or gas boiler?

Absolutely. A Froling pellet boiler connects to your existing central heating pipework and is compatible with radiators, underfloor heating and standard hot water cylinders. The main addition is a fuel store — either a hopper or flexible bag system.

How much does installation cost?

Residential installations typically start from around £8,000–£12,000 before the £5,000 BUS grant. After the grant, the net cost is often comparable to a premium conventional boiler. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote specific to your property.

How often does a pellet boiler need servicing?

An annual service by a qualified HETAS engineer is recommended. The Lambdatronic 3200 provides service reminders and logs all operational data. Freedom Green can connect you with trained Froling service engineers across the UK.
