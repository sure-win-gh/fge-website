Pellet Boilers

pelletstar & pelletstar-H Series

10 – 105 kW

Herz's fully automatic pellet boiler range for residential and light commercial applications. Includes standard pelletstar, pelletstar-H (high efficiency), and pelletstar-HE variants.

  • Ignition elements & electrodes
  • Lambda O₂ sensors & probes
  • Combustion fans & draught motors
  • Pellet auger & feed components
  • Grate bars & cleaning arms
  • Control boards & myHERZ modules
  • Door seals & high-temp gaskets
Pellet Condensing

pelletstar CONDENSATION

80 – 101 kW

Herz's condensing pellet boiler achieving maximum efficiency by recovering latent heat from flue gases. Specialist parts for both the combustion system and the condensing module.

  • Condensing heat exchanger parts
  • Condensate drain & neutralisation kit
  • Lambda sensors & flue components
  • Combustion fan assemblies
  • Grate & ash system components
  • Control board & sensor kits
  • Seals & thermal gaskets
Wood Chip

firematic Series

20 – 301 kW

Herz's flagship wood chip boiler range — robust, high-efficiency, and suitable for a wide range of fuel qualities. Popular for agricultural, commercial, and district heating applications.

  • Firematic grate bars & stoker parts
  • Lambda probes & O₂ sensors
  • Fuel feed auger & screw components
  • Heat exchanger turbulators & baffles
  • Primary & secondary air fans
  • Combustion chamber ceramics & seals
  • Ash auger & removal motors
Log Gasification

firestar Series

18 – 40 kW

Herz log wood gasification boilers. The firestar De Luxe and Lambda ECO variants deliver high-temperature combustion for exceptional efficiency from quality dry logs.

  • Combustion chamber ceramics & linings
  • Secondary air nozzle & rings
  • Door seals & viewing glass
  • Draught fans & fan motors
  • Lambda ECO sensor kits
  • Temperature limiters (STB/STW)
  • Safety valve & sensor components
Combination

pelletfire

20 – 40 kW

The Herz pelletfire combines log wood gasification with automatic pellet burning in a single unit. Parts cover both combustion systems and the integrated control module.

  • Log combustion chamber parts
  • Pellet feed & ignition components
  • Combination control module
  • Shared heat exchanger parts
  • Door seals (log & pellet sides)
  • Lambda sensors & probes
  • Draught fan & motor assembly
Industrial

BioFire & BioFire Taurus

400 – 1,500 kW

Industrial-scale Herz biomass systems for large commercial, district heating, and process applications. Engineered for continuous operation at maximum output with minimal downtime.

  • Heavy-duty industrial grate components
  • High-capacity fuel feed parts
  • Industrial lambda control systems
  • Stoker & retort components
  • Industrial ash removal augers
  • Control system modules & sensors
  • High-temperature combustion seals
